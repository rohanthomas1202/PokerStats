# Privacy Policy for PokerStats

**Last updated:** March 14, 2026

## Overview

PokerStats is a poker session tracking app designed with privacy in mind. Your data belongs to you.

## Data Collection

PokerStats collects only the data you manually enter into the app, including:

- Poker session details (buy-ins, cash-outs, stakes, locations, duration)
- Hand-by-hand action logs (preflop actions, postflop results)
- Mental state ratings (tilt, energy, focus)
- Session notes

**We do not collect:**

- Personal identification information
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Location data (locations are entered manually by you)

## Data Storage

- **On-device:** All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. The app works fully offline.
- **Cloud backup (optional):** If you choose to sign in and enable cloud backup, your session data is stored in Supabase (a secure, open-source database platform). Cloud backup is entirely optional and initiated only by you.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your poker data is never used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than providing the app's functionality to you.

## Third-Party Services

- **Supabase** (optional, for cloud backup only): Subject to [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Apple** (for App Store and authentication): Subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

No advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, or tracking tools are included in the app.

## Data Deletion

- You can delete all your data at any time from the app's Settings screen.
- If you use cloud backup, you can delete your cloud data from the Backup Settings screen.
- You can also delete all data by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

PokerStats is not directed at children under 17 and does not knowingly collect data from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page with an updated revision date.
